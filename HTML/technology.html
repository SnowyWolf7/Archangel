Carbon Engineering

Carbon Engineering is building technologies to capture CO₂ directly from the atmosphere, and to use that CO2 in the synthesis of clean transportation fuels that displace crude oil. Our business is to provide the technology and facilities to synthesize global-scale quantities of clean fuels from air, water, and renewable power.
